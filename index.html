Care Guide

Dust your cabinet regularly with a soft, dry cloth, and use a lightly dampened one only when needed. Keep it away from excess moisture, direct sunlight, and heat to prevent warping or fading. Always lift objects rather than dragging them, and check fittings from time to time to ensure everything stays stable and secure. If storing, make sure the cabinet is fully dry and follow any recommended protective treatments.

Buying Guide

Many of our cabinets feature natural materials - from solid wood to stone or metal accents - so gentle variations in grain, colour, and texture are expected. Lighting and screen settings may also cause slight colour differences. These natural details aren't flaws; they're what make each FableRoom piece feel unique, crafted with care, and truly one of a kind.
